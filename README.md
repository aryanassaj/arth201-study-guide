# ARTH201 Study Guide & Flashcards

Interactive study tools for **ARTH201: History of Western Art II** at the University of Maryland, Spring 2026.

Covers **~163 artworks** from Medieval through Baroque periods (Chapters 16–25).

## What's Included

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [**ARTH201_Study_Guide.pdf**](ARTH201_Study_Guide.pdf) | Clean, printable PDF version of the full study guide — black-and-white, easy to read |
| [**flashcards.html**](flashcards.html) | Interactive flashcards with flip animations, period filters, shuffle, quiz mode, and table view |
| [**study_guide.html**](study_guide.html) | Interactive study guide with search, collapsible sections, color-coded periods, and dark theme |

## How to Use

- **PDF** — Click [ARTH201_Study_Guide.pdf](ARTH201_Study_Guide.pdf) and download. Print it or read on any device.
- **HTML files** — Open the links below directly in your browser (no download needed):
  - [Flashcards](https://aryanassaj.github.io/arth201-study-guide/flashcards.html)
  - [Study Guide](https://aryanassaj.github.io/arth201-study-guide/study_guide.html)
- **Offline** — Click the green "Code" button above, then "Download ZIP". Unzip and open the HTML files in your browser.

## Periods Covered

- Medieval Art (5th–14th C.)
- Northern Renaissance (15th–16th C.)
- Early Italian Renaissance / Quattrocento (15th C.)
- High Renaissance (early 16th C.)
- Mannerism (mid-late 16th C.)
- Baroque — Italy & Spain (17th C.)
- Baroque — Northern Europe (17th C.)

## Features

**Flashcards**
- Flip cards showing artist, title, date, medium, origin, and iconography
- Filter by period or study all at once
- Shuffle, quiz mode, and searchable table view

**Study Guide**
- Artworks organized by period in sortable tables
- Compare/contrast pairs for exam prep
- Artist style signatures for quick identification
- Collapsible sections and full-text search
- Dark theme for comfortable reading
